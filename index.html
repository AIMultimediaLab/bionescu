Short Biography:

He is the General Manager of University "Politehnica" of Bucharest's (UPB), Research Center CAMPUS, and coordinator of the Multimedia Lab. He holds a double PhD in image/video processing from UPB and University of Savoie, France. He is currently a tenured Professor with the Faculty of Electronics, Telecommunications and Information Technology, UPB. He has authored over 180 scientific publications. He serves/served as: reviewer for top tier journals, e.g., IEEE TPAMI, IEEE TIP, IEEE TM, IEEE TCSVT, IEEE SPL, IEEE TAC, ACM CS, lead editor for a Springer ACVPR book, guest editor for journal special issues, e.g., with Image and Vision Computing, Multimedia Tools and Applications, technical programme committee, e.g., with ACM MM, IEEE ICME, ACM ICMR, ECIR, ACM MMSys, conference committee chair, e.g., proceedings/publicity co-chair and area chair @ ACM Multimedia, general chair @ CBMI 2016, ACM ICMR 2017, and CLEF 2021, organizer/co-organizer of workshops, e.g., Information Fusion in Computer Vision for Concept Recognition @ ECCV 2012, Event-based Media Integration and Processing @ ACM MM 2013, Human-Centered Event Understanding from Multimedia @ ACM MM 2014 & 2015, Research and Innovation for Secure Societies @ ICPR 2020, lead organizer/co-organizer for several benchmark campaigns, e.g., MediaEval Retrieving Diverse Social Images (2013-2017), Violent Scenes Detection (2014-2015), Predicting Media Interestingness (2016-2017), Predicting Media Memorability (2018-2020), lead organizer for ImageCLEF (2017-2020), and Multimedia Information Processing for Personality and Social Networks Analysis Challenge @ ICPR 2018. He contributed to over 27 national/international research grants, strategic programmes, and industry projects, out of which to 16 as principal investigator. He is senior member of IEEE and member of SPIE, ACM, EURASIP and GDR-ISIS.
